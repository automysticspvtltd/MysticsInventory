POS (Point of Sale) Requirements
1. Product Search
•	When searching products in POS, show only products available in the POS Store.
•	Do not display products from other warehouses or stores.
•	Search should work by Product Name, SKU, and Barcode.
2. POS Screen Layout
Move fields in the following order:
1.	Mode of Sale
2.	Customer Name
3.	Phone Number
4.	Bag Selection
5.	Payment Method
6.	Order Summary
Mode of Sale
•	Move “Mode of Sale” to the top of the Tender section.
•	Default value should be “POS”.
3. Remove Warehouse
•	Remove the Warehouse dropdown completely from the POS screen.
•	POS should automatically use the configured POS Store stock.
4. Bag Management
•	Bag should be optional, not mandatory.
•	Move Bag selection below the Phone Number field.
•	Allow users to select bag quantity.
•	When bags are added to an order:
o	Deduct the selected bag quantity from stock automatically.
o	Stock should be updated after successful payment.
5. Payment Methods
Keep only:
•	Cash
•	UPI
•	Card
Remove:
•	Bank
•	Other
6. Discounts
Item-wise Discount
Already available and working correctly.
Support:
•	Percentage (%) Discount
•	Fixed Amount Discount
Order-level Discount
Add discount functionality for the entire order.
Support:
•	Percentage (%) Discount
•	Fixed Amount Discount
Examples:
Subtotal: ₹5000
10% Discount → Final Total: ₹4500
₹500 Discount → Final Total: ₹4500
Calculation Flow
Subtotal → Item Discount → Order Discount → Tax → Final Total
Totals should automatically recalculate whenever discounts are applied.
7. Thermal Bill Printing
Create a professional thermal receipt format.
Receipt should include:
Header
•	Company Logo
•	Shop Name
•	Shop Address
•	Phone Number
•	GST Number (if applicable)
Bill Information
•	Invoice Number
•	Date & Time
•	Cashier Name
•	Customer Name
•	Customer Phone Number
Item Details
•	Product Name
•	Quantity
•	Price
•	Item Discount
•	Total Amount
Summary
•	Subtotal
•	Order Discount
•	Tax
•	Grand Total
•	Payment Method
Footer
•	Thank You Message
•	Return/Exchange Policy (Optional)
•	Website or Social Media Details (Optional)
The thermal bill should be properly aligned and optimized for 58mm and 80mm thermal printers.
